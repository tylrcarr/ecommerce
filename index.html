I'm Tyler Carr, the visionary that created this company. I strive every day to help pitchfork lovers everywhere get the forks they need at prices that are affordable to even the least fortunate. I spend a lot of my time programming, playing basketball and working. My hobbies include 2 of those, as well as sleeping at inappropriate times.

Our business was created on April 20th, 2018. We were created with ease of access and low prices in mind, as we strive to uphold the values that the CEO has instilled in us time and time again.

Here are our core values, and what they mean to us:

Passion

What's a pitchfork store without a little passion? We truly do believe in providing quality content, and all of our employees are previous pitchfork users who want to pass on their traditions to the next generation. We strive to provide you the same passion in selling products as you do in using our products.

Service

We know what we're selling. If we don't provide the best service possible, we expect the mob to turn on us.

Learning

We want to inspire the next generation of forkers to use pitchforks in mob rule and enjoy doing so. We venture to help teach new forkers on the priciples of pitchforks at a low cost.

Authenticity

We only sell products that we would use ourselves. What's the point in selling something that you don't use personally?
